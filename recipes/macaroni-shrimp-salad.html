Macaroni-Shrimp Salad

Igredients

  • 4 cup cooked elbow or shell macaroni
  • 5 green onions, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 dill pickles, chopped
  • 1 cup cooked shrimp, or 2 4 1/2 oz. cans
  • 6 hard cooked eggs, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients and mix in mayonnaise to which a small amount of chili sauce has been added.
